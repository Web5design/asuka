archive_package(*args, **kwds)¶

Downloads the source tree and makes the source distribution. It yields triple of package name, filename of the source distribution, and its full path.

with build.archive_package() as (package, filename, path):
    sftp.put(path, filename)
commit = None¶

(Commit) The commit object.

asuka.dist.capture_stdout(*args, **kwds)¶

Captures the standard output (it will get silent) and yields the buffer.

For example, the following prints nothing to console but result becomes 'yeah':

with capture_output() as out:
    print 'yeah'
    result = out.getvalue()
